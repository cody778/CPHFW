Created by two friends in the free-town of Christiania, Copenhagen in 1995, ALIS went from being a local voice to a real movement.

As a voice for youth culture, skateboarding, and streetwear, the brand purposely maintained an air of mystery, committed to staying authentic and underground, built upon pillars of community and a raw desire to create.
