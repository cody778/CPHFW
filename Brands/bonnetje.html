BONNETJE is a Copenhagen-based brand renowned for its innovative approach to fashion, cutting up old suits into entirely new and striking silhouettes. Each creation embodies a dialogue between past craftsmanship and contemporary design, transforming what was once discarded into unique, wearable art.

By thoughtfully reusing defunct materials, BONNETJE actively participates in the pursuit of a fashion culture that moves beyond today’s throwaway mindset. The brand’s work demonstrates a deep commitment to sustainability and conscious design, reflecting a continuous engagement with responsible practices that honor the lessons of the past, address the needs of the present.
