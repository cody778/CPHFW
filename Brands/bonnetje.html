BONNETJE is a Copenhagen-based brand that cuts up old suits and reassembles the pieces into new silhouettes.

By reusing defunct materials, the brand joins the search for fashion beyond today’s throwaway culture. Its work reflects an ongoing engagement with responsible practices of the past, present, and future.
