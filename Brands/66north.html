Designed and proven in Iceland, 66°North is renowned for its durable and functional outerwear. Each garment is carefully crafted to protect against the harsh North Atlantic weather, reflecting the brand’s commitment to practicality and quality.

Founded in 1926 by Hans Kristjánsson, 66°North began by making protective clothing for Icelandic fishermen and workers braving extreme conditions. Over the years, the brand has stayed true to its roots, combining traditional craftsmanship with thoughtful design to meet the demands of both rugged landscapes and everyday life.
