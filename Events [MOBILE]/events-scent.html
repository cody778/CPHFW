Where Art Meets Scent

Haitian artist Manuel Mathieu is launching his first collection of perfumes here.

DESIGNER:Juliette Karagueuzoglou

BRAND: Manuel Mathieu

WHERE: Henrik Vibskov Boutique

WHEN: 16:00 - 27 jan

> MORE ON EVENT
 each bottle a piece of art in itself, crafted with master perfumer Juliette Karagueuzoglou. Expect immersive scents, hand-moulded clay bottles, and a gallery feel—plus a chance to chat with Mathieu about blending art and fragrance. The exhibition runs all week and is as open as the Danish sky.
> MORE ON MANUEL
Manuel Mathieu is a Haitian-born (1986) multidisciplinary artist based in Montréal whose paintings, installations and ceramics interrogate themes of historical violence, spiritual legacy and personal trauma. His work uses an abstract/figurative visual language to explore Haiti's past and his own survival.
