Uniqlo's soufflé house - A sensory escape

The pop-up celebrates the brand's Soufflé Yarn line, and it's a true playground for the senses.

DESIGNER: Vicky Montanari

BRAND: UNIQLO

WHERE: ROOM B, ARENA HALL

WHEN: 09:00 - 27 jan

ADD TO YOUR PICKS
> MORE ON EVENT
Lose yourself in a “scarf forest,” unwind in an ASMR sound lounge, explore a scent library, and grab a drink in the cosiest fashion café in town. There's even a TikTok-ready corner for those chasing the perfect post. It's free, but you'll need the Uniqlo app to nab your slot.
> MORE ON UNIQLO
Uniqlo is a Japanese global apparel retailer known for high quality, affordable and minimalist clothing. It focuses on functional basics rather than fast fashion trends. The brand emphasizes simplicity, comfort and practicality across its collections.
