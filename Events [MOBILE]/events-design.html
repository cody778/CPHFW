Drop in to see how real outerwear is made: live repairs, behind-the-scenes demos, weather testing, and a chance to chat with industry experts about why clothes should last a lifetime. If that's not enough, there's 66°North x Siciliansk IS ice cream on offer, and an inspiring panel talk about fashion's future.
> MORE ON 66°NORTH
An Icelandic outerwear brand founded in 1926 with roots in making life-saving gear for fishermen and search-and-rescue teams. Its offerings blend rugged functionality and everyday urban wear, built on a circular sustainability ethos and a claim to durability lots of brands only talk about.
